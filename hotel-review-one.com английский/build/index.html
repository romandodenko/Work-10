About us

Experience a paradise of tranquility at Meriton Suites Southport, located in a tranquil enclave of the Gold Coast with panoramic views stretching as far as the eye can see.

The hotel's beautifully appointed suites occupy levels 25 to 55, each equipped with all the amenities needed for a relaxing stay. Spacious rooms with luxurious beds and large windows to take in the views invite you to our Southport apartments.
