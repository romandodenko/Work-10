About us

The AthensWas Hotel is also famous for its restaurants and bars. Gourmets can enjoy delicious dishes at the SENSE Fine Dining restaurant, where the chef offers signature cuisine using local and organic products. For lovers of cocktails and a relaxed atmosphere, the hotel offers a rooftop bar with panoramic views of the city and the Acropolis.

Hotel AthensWas also organizes various events and activities for its guests. You can join group excursions to explore the historical sites of Athens, or attend theater performances or concerts organized at the hotel.

For those who want to keep fit and relax, the AthensWas Hotel offers a fitness center with modern equipment. After your workout, guests can visit the spa, where they can enjoy a wide range of treatments and massages for complete relaxation.
