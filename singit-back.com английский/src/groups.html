Groups

The vocal school has several groups engaged in various areas: academic, pop-jazz and folk singing. These groups are among the very first and at the same time among the best in their genres in terms of ratings.

  • Academic singing

    The academic singing group at the vocal school is a group specializing in classical music and opera performance. In this group, students learn the technique of classical singing, working with musical works of various styles and eras, and also receive the basics of music theory. They are trained under the guidance of experienced teachers who are highly qualified in the field of academic singing.

  • Pop-jazz singing

    The pop-jazz group at the vocal school is dedicated to the study of modern genres and styles, such as pop music, jazz, soul and blues. In this group, students master the features of vocal technique in modern music, working with a microphone, arrangement and improvisation. Teachers with solid experience and professional musicians help students develop their own style and expressiveness in pop-jazz music.

  • Folk singing

    The folk singing group at the vocal school is focused on the study and performance of folk songs and music of various peoples and cultures. Students in this group become familiar with the traditions and characteristics of folk music, develop vocal interpretation skills and work with emotional expressiveness. During the learning process, they have the opportunity to immerse themselves in the cultures of different peoples and expand their musical horizons.
